Dr Emmanuel Fernandez

Diligent and enthusiastic Dental Surgeon and Prosthodontist with over fifteen years of experience, coming with outstanding knowledge in performing standard and complex procedures, including cleaning, scaling, quadrant dentistry, fixed and removable prosthodontics, use of Anterior repositioning appliance to eliminate TMD, CAD/CAM crowns for delivery of same day crowns and long-term temporaries, crown preparation and design, rotary endodontics, laser procedures, soft tissue management, frenectomy, fibroma, socket and ridge preservation, root canals, pulpotomy, dental extractions, crown lengthening, osseous surgery, intraoral suturing, CBCT development and interpretation with radiographic stents, performed numerous implant placement surgeries, individual implant crown restorations as well as implant-retained removable prosthetics, limited experience with conventional orthodontics, including treatment planning and bracketing, mentored and trained various dental students 3rd-5th year, postgraduates.

Hardworking and committed to providing patients with a comfortable and successful experience. Adept in pain management and providing patients with guidance on preventative oral hygiene. My aspiration is to work in the United States and keep learning and mastering dentistry art, pursuing Advanced Graduate Program in Implant Dentistry.
